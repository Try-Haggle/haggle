## Style Unification Notes

After generating all sections, use Variant's Style Dropper:

1. Pick the hero section's visual DNA (colors, spacing, typography)
2. Apply to all other sections
3. Key consistency rules:
   - Numbers always monospace, large, bold
   - Green = positive/Haggle, Red = competitor fees, Blue = buyer, Amber = seller
   - Dark backgrounds throughout (navy #0f172a to black #030712)
   - Cards: subtle border, rounded corners, no heavy drop shadows
   - Motion suggestion: numbers should feel like they "tick" up/down
